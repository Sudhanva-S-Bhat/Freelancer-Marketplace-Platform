Why Lumina

Trust is engineered in, not bolted on.

Three systems run under every match on Lumina, quietly doing the work that keeps both sides honest.

Verified identity & skills

Government ID checks and role-specific assessments happen before a freelancer can send a single bid.

Escrow-protected payments

Funds are held the moment a contract starts and released on milestones both sides already agreed to.

Real-time AI matching

New projects are scored against every verified profile the moment they're posted — no waiting for bids to trickle in.
